We say that the [[Functions|function]] $f$ is:
- **Increasing** in the [[Intervals|interval]] $I$ when $f(x_{1}) \lt f(x_{2})$ and $x_{1} \lt x_{2}$
- **Decreasing** in the interval $I$ when $f(x_{1}) \gt f(x_{2})$ and $x_{1} \lt x_{2}$
