We call by Greatest Common Factor the greatest term that appears in all terms of [[Polynomials]]. It's usually the first step on factoring.
$e.g.$
$$\begin{align*}
2x^5+6x^4+4x^3\\
\text{Greatest Common Factor}&= 2x^{3}\\
2x^{3}(x^{2}+3x+2)&= 2x^{3}(x+1)(x+2)
\end{align*}$$
