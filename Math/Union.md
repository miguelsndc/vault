If $S$ and $T$ are [[Sets]], then their union $S \cup T$, is a set that consists of all elements that are in both $S$ or $T$. $e.g.$

Let $S = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5\}$
Let $T= \{ 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8\}$

Therefore, $S\cup T = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5 ,6, 7 ,8\}$
