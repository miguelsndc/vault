Table of immediate trigonometric [[Antiderivative of a function|integral]]s:
- $\int \cos{x} \, dx= \sin{x} + C$
- $\int \sin{x} \, dx = -\cos{x} \, + C$
- $\int \sec{x}\tan{x} \, dx = \sec{x} + C$ 
- $\int \sec^{2}{x} \, dx = \tan{x} + C$.
- $\int -\csc^{2}{x} = \cot{x} + C$.
- $\int \tan{x} \, dx = -\ln{\abs{\cos{x}}} + C$.
