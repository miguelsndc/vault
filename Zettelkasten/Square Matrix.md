A **Square Matrix** is a special kind of [[Matrix Definition|matrix]], where the numbers of rows is equal to the number of columns, $(m = n)$, See:
$$\begin{align*}A_{3\times 3}&= 
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
8 & 9 & 32\\
\sin{45\degree}  & e^{x} & \phi
\end{bmatrix}
\end{align*}$$
