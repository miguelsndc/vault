Let $m$, $n$, $r$ be nonnegative integers with $r$ not exceeding either $m$ or $n$, then:
$$\begin{align*}
{m+n\choose r}=\sum\limits_{k=0}^{n}{m\choose r-k}{n\choose k}
\end{align*}$$
