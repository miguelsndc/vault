The [[Field|field]] axioms are the set of rules defined for addition and multiplication that make a [[Sets|set]] a field.
The following rules are defined for addition and multiplication respectively.
- Associativity
$$\begin{align*}
(a+b)+c&= a+(b+c)\\
(ab)c&= a(bc)
\end{align*}$$
- Commutativity
$$\begin{align*}
a+b&= b+a\\
ab&= ba
\end{align*}$$
- Distributivity
$$\begin{align*}
a(b+c)&= ab+ac\\
(a+b)c&= ac+bc
\end{align*}$$
- Identity 
$$\begin{align*}
a+0&= a=0+a\\
a\cdot1&= a=1\cdot a
\end{align*}$$
- Inverses
$$\begin{align*}
a+(-a)&= 0=(-a)+a\\
a\cdot a^{-1}&= 1=a^{-1}a
\end{align*}$$
