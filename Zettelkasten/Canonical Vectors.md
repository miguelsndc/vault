The vectors with [[vault/content/S1/AVLC/Vector Norm|magnitude]] one $1$, that falls each on a coordinate axis. They are called $i, j, k$ respectively for the axis $(x,y,z)$. See:
$$\begin{align*}
i &= (1,0,0)\\
j&= (0,1,0)\\
k&= (0,0,1)
\end{align*}$$
