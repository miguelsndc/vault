Theorem: Let $f(x)$ be a differentiable function, then:
- $f(x) = x^{n} \implies f'(x) = nx^{n-1}$
- $f(x)= x^{-n}\implies f'(x) = -nx^{-n-1}$
- $f(x)= x^{\frac{1}{n}} \implies f'(x) = \frac{1}{x} x^{\frac{1}{n}-1}$
