>[!tip] Sine and Cosine of Difference:
>From [[Euler's Formula]] we derive:
>$$\begin{align*}
\sin(A-B)&= \sin(A)\cos(B)-\cos(A)\sin(B)\\
\cos(A-B)&= \cos(A)\cos(B)+\sin(A)\sin(B)
\end{align*}$$
