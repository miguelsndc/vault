The point-slope form of a line equation is denoted by:
$$\begin{align*}
(y-y_{0}) =m(x-x_{0})
\end{align*}$$
Where $y_{0}$ and $x_{0}$ are a point of the line and $m$ is the [[Average Rate of Change|slope]].
