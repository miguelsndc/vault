A exponential function is a [[Functions|function]] that assumes the form:
$$\begin{align*}
f(x)&= a^{x}
\end{align*}$$
Where $a \gt 0$ is a [[Real Numbers|real number]] and $x \ne 1$, because if $x$ were $1$ then it would be just a constant function.
