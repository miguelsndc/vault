To multiply a [[Vector]] $\vec{a} = \{a_{1}, a_{2}, a_{3}\}$ by a scalar quantity $\lambda$, we do:
$$\begin{align*}
\vec{b} &= \begin{cases}
b_{1}&= \lambda a_{1}\\
b_{2}&= \lambda a_{2}\\
b_{3}&= \lambda a_{3}
\end{cases}
\end{align*}$$
