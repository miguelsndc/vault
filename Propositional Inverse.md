The inverse is a **compound proposition** derived from the [[Conditional Statement]], It's denoted as $\neg p \implies \neg q$.
##### Truth Table
| p   | q   | $p \implies q$ | $\neg p \implies \neg q$ |
| --- | --- | -------------- | ------------------------ |
| F   | F   | T              | T                        |
| F   | T   | T              | F                        |
| T   | F   | F              | T                        |
| T   | T   | T              | T                         |
