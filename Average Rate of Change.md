The average rate of change of a [[Functions|function]] $y=f(x)$ between $x=a$ and $x=b$ is:
$$\begin{align*}
\text{average rate of change}&= \frac{\text{change in y}}{\text{change in x}}=f(b)-f(a)
\end{align*}$$
