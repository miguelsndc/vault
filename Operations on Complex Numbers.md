Operations with [[Complex Numbers]] are perford similarly to operations with expressions of the form $a + b\sqrt{c}$, but keep in mid that $i^{2} = -1$

1. Addition
$$\begin{align*}
(a+bi) + (c + di) &= (a+c)+(b+d)i
\end{align*}$$
2. Subtraction
$$
\begin{align*}
(a+bi)-(c+di)&= (a-c) + (b-d)i
\end{align*}
$$
3. Multiplication
$$\begin{align*}
(a+bi)\cdot(c+di) &= (ac-bd) + (ad + bc)i
\end{align*}$$
